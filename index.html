About us

One Piece is a Japanese anime and manga series about Monkey D. Luffy, a boy whose body became rubber after eating a Devil Fruit, and his pirate crew, the Straw Hat Pirates. Together, they sail the seas in search of the legendary treasure known as the "One Piece" to help Luffy become the next King of the Pirates. Along their journey, they overcome various challenges and form bonds of friendship.
